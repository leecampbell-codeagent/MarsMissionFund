05 — Voice

Voice & Tone

Mars Mission Fund speaks like a brilliant, optimistic engineer who also happens to be brilliant at making complex things feel exciting to anyone.

✓ Do write this way
  • Back the team building pressurized habitats for the first Mars crews
  • 73% funded — 18 days left to join the mission
  • Every $50 moves the launch window closer
  • Your stake. Their mission. Our planet.
  • Funding opens March 1. Liftoff begins March 21.
✕ Avoid this language
  • Please consider investing in our exciting new venture opportunity
  • Utilizing synergistic technologies for next-gen planetary colonization
  • Click here to learn more about our financial product
  • Results may not be as depicted. This is not investment advice.
  • Our revolutionary disruptive space platform solutions
